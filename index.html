About Sophia Martinez

With over 15 years of experience in interior design, I believe that great design is about more than aesthetics—it's about creating spaces that tell stories and enhance the way we live and work.

My approach is rooted in timeless design principles, deep client collaboration, and meticulous attention to detail. I work closely with each client to understand their vision, lifestyle, and needs, translating those insights into spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

Education & Experience

  • Bachelor of Fine Arts, Interior Design - Rhode Island School of Design
  • 15+ years professional experience
  • Licensed Interior Designer, California & New York
  • LEED Accredited Professional

Awards & Recognition
